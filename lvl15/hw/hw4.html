mechanical keyboard

description

A mechanical keyboard uses individual mechanical switches beneath each key to register keystrokes, providing tactile feedback and audible sound. These keyboards are known for their durability, with switches rated for tens of millions of key presses, making them ideal for heavy typists and gamers . The customization options for mechanical switches allow users to choose between various types, such as linear, tactile, and clicky, to match their preferences. Additionally, mechanical keyboards often feature RGB lighting, programmable keys, and ergonomic designs, enhancing both aesthetics and functionality.

headset

A headset is a device that combines headphones and a microphone into one unit, designed for hands-free communication and immersive audio experiences. It is commonly used for gaming, video conferencing, and content creation, providing clear audio output and input. Many headsets offer noise-canceling features to block out background noise, ensuring a focused and high-quality sound experience. With adjustable headbands, cushioned ear pads, and built-in controls, headsets are designed for comfort and convenience during extended use.
