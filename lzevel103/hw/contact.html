Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Goa Academy

What are the admission requirements? +

We require a high school diploma or equivalent. No prior coding experience is necessary, but strong problem-solving skills and dedication are essential. All applicants complete a brief assessment and interview as part of the admissions process.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we offer several financing options including payment plans, income share agreements, and scholarships for qualified candidates. Our admissions team can help you find the best option for your situation.

What is the class schedule like? +

Our programs are full-time and intensive, typically running Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 5 PM. We also offer part-time evening options for those who need more flexibility.

Do you provide job placement assistance? +

Absolutely! Our career services team provides comprehensive support including resume reviews, interview preparation, portfolio building, and connections to our network of hiring partners. We have a 95% job placement rate within 6 months of graduation.

Can I visit the campus before applying? +

Yes, we welcome prospective students to visit our campus. We offer weekly information sessions and campus tours. Please contact our admissions team to schedule your visit.
